Solving the issue from a different, wider perspective.

Surveillance is an integral part of security and control.

Requires extended periods of examining multiple footages for evidence. This manual task provides evidence in court but is rarely used to detect and prevent crime in real-time. We bring a solution to ensure a step towards safety of women and reduce the crime statistics.

In Delhi

  • 11,542 cases of crime in 2017
  • 1,05,000 CCTV cameras are installed in public places.
  • 1,40,000 CCTV cameras are in the process of installation.

We convert an evidence collecting tool to a crime prevention and detection tool.
